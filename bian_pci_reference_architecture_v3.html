          PCI DSS v4.0.1 — All 12 core requirements applicable to the CDE of this architecture. Standard last updated: March 2024.
          Requirements 1 & 2
          Network security & infrastructure
          MandatoryNGFW segmenting the CDE from the corporate network and DMZ
          MandatoryProhibit direct remote access to CDE without encrypted VPN + MFA + PAM
          CriticalDisable unnecessary services, protocols and ports on all CDE systems
          MandatoryReview firewall rules every 6 months; justify each rule with a change ticket
          Requirement 3
          Protection of stored cardholder data
          MandatoryNever store CVV2, PIN or magnetic stripe data after authorisation
          MandatoryPAN stored only with truncation (last 4 digits visible) or irreversible tokenisation
          MandatoryAES-256 encryption for CHD at rest; keys in HSM FIPS 140-2 L3
          CriticalMinimum retention policy: purge CHD when no longer required for a legitimate business purpose
          Requirement 4
          Encryption of CHD in transmission
          MandatoryTLS 1.3 exclusively for CHD transmission; TLS 1.0/1.1 explicitly disabled
          MandatoryCertificate validation with Certificate Transparency and mandatory OCSP stapling
          CriticalProhibit sending unprotected PANs via messaging, email, chat or open channels
          Requirements 5 & 6
          Anti-malware & secure development
          MandatoryAntivirus/EDR on all CDE systems; daily signature updates
          MandatorySAST + DAST + SCA in CI/CD pipeline; block deployments with critical vulnerabilities
          CriticalOWASP Top 10 + SANS CWE Top 25 as minimum secure coding requirements
          MandatoryAuthorised software inventory (SBOM); block execution of unauthorised software
          Requirements 7 & 8
          Access control & identity
          MandatoryNeed-to-know: CHD access only when required by role; deny by default
          MandatoryMandatory MFA for remote access, administrator accounts and CDE systems
          MandatoryPasswords: minimum 12 characters, complexity, rotation every 90 days, history of 12
          CriticalDisable accounts inactive >90 days; certify privileged access quarterly
          Requirement 9
          Physical security of the CDE
          MandatoryPhysical access control to data centre with biometrics or smart card; entry log maintained
          MandatoryVisitors escorted in CDE zones; access log retained for minimum 3 months
          CriticalMonthly anti-skimming inspection of all POS/ATM terminals in the bank's own network
          StandardSecure media destruction for CHD-bearing assets: destruction certificate required
          Requirements 10 & 11
          Monitoring, logging & testing
          MandatoryCentralised logging of all CHD accesses, configuration changes and security events
          MandatoryIDS/IPS at CDE perimeter and interior; real-time SOC alerts with SLA <15min
          MandatoryWeekly internal scanning; quarterly external ASV; annual pen test (web + network)
          CriticalFIM (File Integrity Monitoring) to detect unauthorised changes to critical CDE files
          Requirement 12
          Security policies & governance
          MandatoryInformation security policy approved by senior management; mandatory annual review
          MandatoryAnnual security awareness programme for all personnel; completion records maintained
          MandatoryDocumented and tested incident response plan; annual CHD breach simulation exercise
          CriticalThird-party management: inventory of vendors with CHD access; signed PCI DSS agreements
          MandatoryAnnual documented risk assessment; designated PCI compliance officer (ISA or QSA)
